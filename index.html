Ibadan, Nigeria

Ibadan, the third largest city in Nigeria, is renowned for its rich historical and cultural heriatge. Located in the southwestern part of the country, it's known for its sprawling landscapes, ancient landmark like the Cocoa House, and Unversity of Ibadan, Nigeria's premier university. Nigeria itself is a vibrant nation in West Africa, celebrated for its diverse cultures, languages, and booming entertainment industry, particularly Nollywood and Afrobeats music. The counrty is a mosaic of ethnic groups, each contributing to its dynamic and spirited national identity.
