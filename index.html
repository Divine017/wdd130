Ibadan, Nigeria

Ibadan, the third largest city in Nigeria, is renowned for its rich historical and cultural heritage. Located in the southwestern part of the country, it's known for its sprawling landscapes, ancient landmarks like the Cocoa House, and University of Ibadan, Nigeria's premier university. Nigeria itself is a vibrant nation in West Africa, celebrated for its diverse cultures, languages, and booming entertainment industry, particularly Nollywood and Afrobeats music. The country is a mosaic of ethnic groups, each contributing to its dynamic and spirited national identity.
